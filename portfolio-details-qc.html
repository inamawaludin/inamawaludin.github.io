Manufacturing Quality Control

Manufacturing Quality Control, also known as Quality Control in Manufacturing, refers to the processes and activities implemented by manufacturers to ensure that products meet specified quality standards and customer requirements. It involves systematic inspections, tests, and evaluations conducted at various stages of the manufacturing process to identify and address any defects or deviations from desired quality parameters.

Following are the features and modules contained in this application:

  • Defect Prevention
  • Conformance to Specifications
  • Consistency and Uniformity
  • Incoming Inspection
  • Mixing Inspection
  • Packaging Inspection
